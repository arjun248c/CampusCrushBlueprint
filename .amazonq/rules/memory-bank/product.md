# Campus Crush - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Campus Crush is a college-focused social rating platform that allows students to discover and rate their peers in a gamified, privacy-conscious environment. The application combines elements from popular apps like Tinder (card interactions), Instagram (profile grids), and Duolingo (leaderboards) to create an engaging campus social experience.

## Key Features

### Core Rating System
- **Anonymous Rating Interface**: Students can rate peers on a 1-10 scale with complete anonymity
- **Card-Based Discovery**: Tinder-style card interface for browsing and rating profiles
- **Grid View Discovery**: Instagram-inspired grid layout for profile exploration
- **Quick Rating Actions**: Floating buttons for rapid rating interactions

### Gamification Elements
- **Dynamic Leaderboards**: Real-time rankings showing top-rated students with podium-style display
- **Achievement System**: Visual feedback and progress indicators for user engagement
- **Statistics Dashboard**: Personal metrics including total ratings received and average scores
- **Rank Badges**: Special recognition for top performers with medal icons and glow effects

### Social Features
- **Profile Management**: Comprehensive user profiles with photos and basic information
- **Discovery Filters**: Advanced filtering options for finding specific types of profiles
- **Activity Feeds**: Timeline of recent ratings and interactions
- **Verification System**: Blue checkmark verification for authentic student profiles

### Privacy & Safety
- **Anonymous Interactions**: All ratings are completely anonymous to protect user privacy
- **Minimal Data Exposure**: Profile cards show only essential information
- **Report System**: Built-in reporting mechanism for inappropriate content
- **Admin Moderation**: Comprehensive admin dashboard for content management

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **College Students**: Main user base seeking social connections and peer recognition
- **Campus Communities**: Students looking to engage with their immediate college environment
- **Social Enthusiasts**: Users interested in gamified social interactions

### Use Cases
- **Peer Discovery**: Finding and connecting with other students on campus
- **Social Validation**: Receiving feedback and recognition from peers
- **Campus Networking**: Building social connections within the college community
- **Entertainment**: Casual browsing and rating as a social activity

## Value Proposition
Campus Crush provides a unique blend of social discovery, gamification, and privacy protection specifically designed for college environments. Unlike generic dating apps, it focuses on peer recognition and campus community building while maintaining user anonymity and safety.
